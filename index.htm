With the eruption of doge tokens, we are yet to see doge tokens with utility.
This is where Astro Doge comes in. Astro Doge is here to revolutionize the doge
world by bridging the gaps in the metaverse and creating utility.

metaswap

MetaSwap is the native decentralized exchange of the Astro Doge project. This easy-to-use platform will be a simple way for newcomers to buy $MSHIBA using a multitude of wallets and the ability to buy other tokens on the platform. With future developments, users will be able to buy $MSHIBA directly from third parties using different types of payments. Not only will users be able to view newly listed tokens, but they will also be able to see the Top Gainers in the last 15 min, 1 hr and 24 hrs, giving users an advantage to be the first to invest into the newly listed tokens.

metatrade

MetaTrade is a charting system which brings information update live and allows Astro Doge users to execute their cryptocurrency orders at a doge-fast speed. Users will be able to view their charts on the Dapp and through an integration with MetaSwap, will be able to purchase cryptocurrencies instantly.

metatreats

Earn great treats by staking your $MSHIBA. Hodlers of $MSHIBA can stake their MSHIBA in staking pools whereby they will be rewarded with high APY. They can also act as liquidity providers and be rewarded based on the amount of LP tokens that they hold.

metawallet

The meta wallet is a revolutionized wallet that will allow doge lovers to hodl their tokens. This wallet will initially be functionable within the Astro Doge ecosystem but with future development will be available for use across multiple platforms.

metamerch

$MSHIBA brings real world utilities by allowing hoomans to buy merchandise from the exclusive Astro Doge Boutique. Merchandise can be bought using $ADOGE.

Meta Foundation

Astro Doge loves to spread love, be it in the metaverse or the physical world. The Astro Doge Foundation will be donating regularly to dog shelters to help provide a better world for our comrades.

Astro Doge NFT Game

The multiplayer mode of the Astro Doge game is a Play-To-Earn NFT-Based game. Player must purchase a Astro Doge NFT character and use this character in the multiplayer game. Play-To Earn minigames within the game will allow hodlers to increase their in-game holding amount which in turn can be converted to $MSHIBA. The game will incorporate multiple features such as player vs player where players can use their Astro Doge NFT against other players. The attributes are based on the rarity of the NFT which are defined in the metadata of each NFT. Other features within the game will include the need to complete daily tasks amongst others. The Astro Doge NFT platform will allow users to check the strength level of their Astro Doge with different traits bringing different powers. With a limited amount of Astro Doge NFTs , Astro Doge will become scarce which is when the in-game and on-market trading begins.
